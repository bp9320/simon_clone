Simon Clone

Welcome to my color pattern memorization game!

This game came about when trying to come up with ideas for projects to build to practice my JavaScript. When thinking about games from the past that would be fun to clone, the classic pattern matching game Simon came to mind. After mentioning the idea to my kid who gave it a thumbs up, it was time to get to work.

How to Play

  • After reading the instructions, click the start button to reveal the game.
  • A 3 second countdown will run, then the first colored circle will appear.
  • When the pattern display circle says "GO!", click on the circle with the same color that was previously displayed.
  • If you clicked the correct colored circle, pattern display circle will fade out and the pattern will play again, adding an additional, randomly selected color to the end of the pattern each time.
  • If you click on an incorrect circle, the game will end, and you will be notified of how many rounds you successfully completed.
